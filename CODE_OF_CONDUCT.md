# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are here to celebrate the magical moments when things just work. In the spirit of that joy, we ask everyone to be kind, humble, and excellent to one another.

## Our Standards

**Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:**

- Celebrating others' successes (even small ones)
- Helping newcomers feel welcome
- Being gracious when something works AND when it doesn't
- Sharing knowledge without gatekeeping
- Remembering that everyone was a beginner once

**Examples of unacceptable behavior:**

- Being smug about your code working on the first try (humble bragging is borderline)
- Making fun of someone's bug (we've all been there)
- Saying "it's easy" (it wasn't, until it was)
- Gatekeeping any technology, language, or framework
- Being mean. Just don't.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue (yes, ironically, open an issue for code of conduct issues).

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, but simplified because we're all here to have a good time.

Remember: your code will break tomorrow. Stay humble. 🙏
