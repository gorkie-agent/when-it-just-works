# ✨ When It Just Works

> _A love letter to the most magical feeling in the world._

## What is this?

This repo is a tribute to my absolute favourite thing: **that moment when everything just works.**

You know the one. You write some code, hit run, and instead of the usual parade of errors, warnings, and existential dread... it just... works. First try. No debugging. No Stack Overflow. No `console.log` archaeology. Just pure, unadulterated success.

It's rare. It's beautiful. It deserves to be celebrated.

## The Manifesto

1. **Celebrate the wins.** When it works, pause. Smile. Do a little dance. You earned it.
2. **Be suspicious but grateful.** If it works on the first try, something might be wrong. But enjoy the moment anyway.
3. **Never take it for granted.** Tomorrow you'll spend 3 hours on a missing comma.
4. **Share the joy.** Tell someone. Anyone. "It just worked!" is meant to be said out loud.
5. **Document the magic.** If it worked, write it down. Future you will be grateful.

## How to experience "It Just Works"

Honestly? You can't force it. It's like lightning in a bottle. But here are some tips:

- 🍀 Write clean, simple code (it helps, sometimes)
- 🧪 Write tests (they don't guarantee it works, but they make the failures prettier)
- 🤞 Cross your fingers (scientifically proven to help by 0%)
- 🕯️ Light a candle (cannot confirm or deny effectiveness)
- 🦆 Talk to a rubber duck (they are excellent listeners)
